# Founder's Oath

As founders of Aura-Nova Tech Studio, we commit to building technology that serves people with integrity, professionalism, and excellence.

We will pursue long-term value over short-term gain.

We will protect the trust placed in us by our customers, partners, and communities.

We will document our knowledge, challenge our assumptions, learn continuously, and leave every system better than we found it.

We accept responsibility for the technology we create and the impact it has on society.

This is our commitment to the future of Aura-Nova.
